# PTO Policy

## Overview
Our company provides generous paid time off to support work-life balance and employee well-being.

## Annual Allowance

### Vacation Time
- **15 days** of vacation time per calendar year
- Pro-rated for new hires based on start date
- Accrual begins immediately upon hire

### Sick Leave
- **10 days** of sick leave per calendar year
- Can be used for personal illness or family care
- Doctor's note required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days

### Holidays
- 10 company-observed holidays annually
- Additional floating holiday (employee's choice)

## Request Process

### How to Request PTO

1. **Submit Request in Workday**
   - Navigate to Time Off > Request Time Off
   - Select date range and PTO type (vacation or sick)
   - Add brief note if needed

2. **Advance Notice Requirements**
   - Vacation: Minimum 2 weeks advance notice
   - Sick leave: Notify manager ASAP, submit in Workday within 24 hours
   - Last-minute emergencies: Contact manager directly first

3. **Approval Workflow**
   - Request goes to direct manager for approval
   - Manager has 3 business days to approve/deny
   - Email notification sent upon decision
   - Approved time appears on team calendar automatically

## Blackout Periods

Vacation requests may be restricted during:
- End of fiscal quarter (last 2 weeks of March, June, September, December)
- Major product launches or customer events
- Peak business season (varies by department)

Managers will communicate blackout dates at least 4 weeks in advance.

## Carryover Rules

- **Vacation**: Up to 5 unused days can roll over to next calendar year
- **Sick Leave**: No limit on accrual (does not expire)
- Vacation days exceeding 5 will be paid out in final December paycheck

## Extended Leave

For extended absences beyond accrued PTO:
- **FMLA**: Up to 12 weeks unpaid leave (qualifying events)
- **Parental Leave**: Separate 12-week paid policy (see Parental Leave Guide)
- **Sabbatical**: Available after 5 years tenure (see Sabbatical Policy)
