# Work From Home & Hybrid Policy

## Overview

We believe in flexible work arrangements that balance productivity, collaboration, and work-life integration. This policy outlines eligibility, expectations, and approval processes for remote and hybrid work.

## Hybrid Work Model

### Standard Hybrid Schedule

**Default for most roles:** 3 days in office, 2 days remote per week

**Core In-Office Days (requires team presence):**
- Tuesday: Team collaboration day
- Thursday: All-hands meetings and cross-team collaboration

**Flexible Days (employee choice):**
- Monday, Wednesday, Friday: Work from home or office

**Your Team's Schedule:** Confirm with your manager for team-specific norms.

### Fully Remote Roles

Some positions qualify for 100% remote:
- Technical roles with minimal in-person collaboration requirements
- Positions serving distributed customers/teams
- Employees living 100+ miles from office
- Approval required from Director+ level

## Eligibility

### Who Can Work Remotely?

**Eligible:**
- Full-time employees past 90-day probationary period
- Performance rating "Meets Expectations" or higher
- Role does not require physical presence (e.g., not facilities, front desk, lab work)
- Manager approval

**Not Eligible:**
- New hires in first 90 days (need onboarding immersion)
- Performance improvement plan participants
- Roles requiring on-site equipment or security

## Approval Process

### Request Hybrid/Remote Arrangement

1. **Discuss with Manager**
   - Explain desired schedule (2 days remote, 3 days remote, fully remote)
   - Address how you'll maintain productivity and collaboration
   - Discuss any concerns

2. **Submit Formal Request**
   - Navigate to Workday > Actions > Request Flexible Work Arrangement
   - Select schedule type
   - Provide business justification
   - Manager reviews within 5 business days

3. **Trial Period**
   - Initial approval for 90-day trial
   - Monthly check-ins with manager
   - Evaluate productivity and collaboration
   - Either permanentize or adjust

### Changing Your Schedule

- Remote schedule is a privilege, not a right
- Manager can revoke with 2 weeks notice if business needs change
- Employee can request change at any time (subject to approval)

## Equipment & Stipend

### Company-Provided Equipment

All remote workers receive:
- **Laptop**: MacBook Pro or Dell XPS (IT-managed)
- **Monitor**: 27" external display (optional but recommended)
- **Keyboard & Mouse**: Wireless set
- **Headset**: Noise-canceling for video calls
- **Webcam**: 1080p (if laptop camera insufficient)

**Setup:** IT ships to your home address. Return upon separation or remote privilege revoked.

### Home Office Stipend

**Annual Allowance:** $500 per calendar year

**Eligible Expenses:**
- Desk and ergonomic chair
- Desk lamp or lighting
- Monitor arm or laptop stand
- Cable management
- Printer/scanner (if job requires)
- Wi-Fi range extender

**Not Covered:**
- Internet service (employee responsibility)
- Rent or utilities
- Decorative items
- Personal electronics

**Reimbursement Process:**
1. Purchase eligible items (keep receipts)
2. Submit expense report in Workday
3. Attach itemized receipts
4. Select expense type: "Home Office Equipment"
5. Reimbursed within 2 weeks

**One-Time Setup:** Year 1 you get $500. Year 2+ you get $200/year for replacements.

## Expectations & Requirements

### Work Hours & Availability

- **Core Hours:** 10am-3pm local time (must be available for meetings)
- **Response Time:** Respond to messages within 2 hours during core hours
- **Calendar:** Keep Workday calendar updated with WFH days
- **Time Zone:** Notify team if working from different time zone

### Communication & Collaboration

**Required:**
- Video ON for all meetings (builds connection)
- Slack/Teams active during work hours
- Attend all scheduled team meetings (no "remote skip")
- Proactive communication (over-communicate vs. under-communicate)

**Best Practices:**
- Use status indicators ("In a meeting", "Focusing", "Away")
- Respond to @mentions promptly
- Document decisions in writing (don't rely on hallway convos you miss)

### Productivity Standards

Performance measured by **outcomes, not hours logged**:
- Meet deadlines and deliverables
- Maintain quality of work
- Participate actively in meetings
- Make progress on goals

**If productivity concerns arise:**
- Manager initiates conversation
- Develop improvement plan
- May adjust remote schedule or require office presence

### Security & Confidentiality

**Required:**
- Use company VPN when accessing systems from home
- Lock laptop when stepping away
- No sharing devices with family members
- Secure home office (no confidential conversations in public spaces like coffee shops)
- Report security incidents immediately to IT

## Home Office Setup

Prevent injury with proper setup:
- **Monitor:** Eye level, arm's length away
- **Chair:** Adjustable height, lumbar support, feet flat on floor
- **Keyboard/Mouse:** Elbows at 90Â°, wrists neutral
- **Lighting:** Natural light if possible, reduce glare on screen

**Minimum Speeds:**
- Download: 25 Mbps

- Low latency (<50ms ping)

**Test your speed:** speedtest.net

**Insufficient speed?** Contact IT about hotspot or internet supplement options.

## Working from Locations Other Than Home

### Temporary Remote Work from Other Location

Need to work from a different city temporarily?

**Allowed:**
- Visiting family for 1-2 weeks
- Digital nomad experience
- Extended travel

**Approval Required:**
- Submit request in Workday > Request Location Change
- Requires manager approval
- VP approval if international location
- Maximum 4 weeks per location without tax implications

**Considerations:**
- Time zone differences (must attend team meetings)
- Internet reliability
- Workspace quality

### International Remote Work

**Complex tax and immigration issues.** Requires:
- 30+ days advance notice
- Legal and Tax team review
- VP+ approval
- May not be feasible depending on country

Contact: globalhr@company.com

## Parents & Caregivers

### Flexibility for Caregiving

We understand caregiving responsibilities:
- Flexible start/end times (with manager approval)
- Split shift options (e.g., work 8-12, break for pickup, work 2-6)
- Use PTO for appointments and sick kids

**Not Allowed:**
- Working while primary caregiver for child/dependent without other care
- Remote work is not a substitute for childcare

## Collaboration & Team Building

### Maintaining Culture Remotely

**Required In-Office Events:**
- Quarterly all-hands (dates announced 60 days ahead)
- Annual team offsites
- Department meetings (monthly)

**Virtual Team Building:**
- Virtual coffee chats (15-min 1:1s)
- Team trivia or game sessions
- Virtual lunch & learns

**Travel Required:** If fully remote, expect 4-6 office visits/year for key events. Company covers travel costs.

## FAQs

**Q: Can I work from a coffee shop?**
A: Yes, occasionally. Ensure secure connection (use VPN), avoid discussing confidential info aloud, and check productivity/call quality.

**Q: Do I need to tell my manager if I go into the office on a remote day?**
A: Not required, but update calendar so team knows you're there. Great for spontaneous collaboration!

**Q: What if my internet goes down at home?**
A: Backup options: mobile hotspot, nearby coffee shop, or come into office. Use PTO if unable to work.

**Q: Can remote workers get promoted?**
A: Absolutely. Promotions based on performance, not location. Make your work visible through documentation and proactive communication.

**Q: What if my role becomes ineligible for remote work?**
A: Company provides 60 days notice if role changes due to business needs. Work with manager on transition plan.
