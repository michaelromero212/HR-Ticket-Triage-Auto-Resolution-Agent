# Workday How-To Guide

## Getting Started

### Logging In

**Web Browser:**
- Go to: workday.company.com
- Enter: Company email and password
- Setup MFA on first login (required)

**Mobile App:**
- Download "Workday" app from App Store or Google Play
- Company ID: **COMPANY123**
- Use same email/password as web

### Password Reset

**If you remember your current password:**
1. Log in to Workday
2. Click profile icon (top right)
3. Select **Security Settings**
4. Click **Change Password**
5. Enter current password, then new password (twice)
6. Password requirements: 12+ characters, uppercase, lowercase, number, symbol

**If you forgot your password:**
1. Go to workday.company.com
2. Click **Forgot Password?**
3. Enter company email
4. Check email for reset link (valid 1 hour)
5. Click link and create new password
6. If no email received, check spam or contact IT: x4357

## Updating Personal Information

### Change Your Address

1. Navigate to **Personal Information**
2. Click **Contact** tab
3. Find "Home Address" section
4. Click **Edit** (pencil icon)
5. Enter new address
6. Click **Submit**
7. HR approves within 1 business day

**Important:** Address changes affect tax withholding and benefits. Update before end of year for tax accuracy.

### Update Phone Number or Emergency Contact

1. Navigate to **Personal Information**
2. Click **Contact** tab
3. Find "Phone Numbers" or "Emergency Contacts"
4. Click **Edit**
5. Add/update information
6. Click **Submit** (no approval needed)

### Change Direct Deposit

1. Navigate to **Pay**
2. Click **Payment Elections**
3. Click **Add** to add new bank account
4. Enter:
   - Bank routing number (9 digits)
   - Account number
   - Account type (checking or savings)
5. Attach voided check or bank letter (required)
6. Allocate % or $ amount to each account
7. Submit for approval
8. Changes effective next pay period (if submitted 5+ days before payday)

**Pro Tip:** Keep old account active until you receive one direct deposit to new account.

## Viewing Pay Information

### View Pay Stubs

1. Navigate to **Pay**
2. Click **View Payslips**
3. Select pay period from dropdown
4. Click **View** to see details
5. Click **Print** to download PDF

Pay stubs available 24 hours before payday (every other Friday).

### View W-2 Tax Documents

1. Navigate to **Pay**
2. Click **Tax Documents**
3. Select tax year
4. Click **W-2** to view/download
5. Available by January 31

**Mailing:** Workday automatically sends W-2 to address on file by Jan 31. Update address in December to ensure delivery.

### View Year-to-Date (YTD) Earnings

1. Navigate to **Pay**
2. Click **View Payslips**
3. Latest pay stub shows YTD totals:
   - Gross earnings
   - Total deductions
   - Net pay
   - Federal/state tax withheld
   - 401(k) contributions

## Requesting Time Off

### Submit PTO Request

1. Navigate to **Time Off**
2. Click **Request Time Off**
3. Select **Time Off Type**:
   - Vacation
   - Sick Leave
   - Personal Day
4. Select **From Date** and **To Date**
5. Enter total hours/days
6. Add note (optional but recommended)
7. Review summary
8. Click **Submit**

**Timeline:**
- Manager notified immediately
- Approval/denial within 3 business days
- You receive email confirmation
- Approved time auto-populates team calendar

### View PTO Balance

1. Navigate to **Time Off**
2. Click **View Time Off Balances**
3. See remaining balances for:
   - Vacation days
   - Sick leave
   - Floating holidays
4. View accrual schedule and carryover

**Note:** Balances update in real-time as you submit requests and use time off.

### Cancel PTO Request

1. Navigate to **Time Off**
2. Click **My Time Off**
3. Find pending or approved request
4. Click request, then **Cancel Request**
5. Enter reason (sent to manager)
6. Click **Submit Cancellation**

## Managing Benefits

### View Current Benefits

1. Navigate to **Benefits**
2. Click **My Benefits**
3. See current elections:
   - Medical, dental, vision plans
   - Life insurance coverage
   - Disability insurance
   - FSA balances
   - 401(k) contribution rate

### Change 401(k) Contribution

Can change anytime (not just during open enrollment):

1. Navigate to **Benefits**
2. Click **Retirement Plans**
3. Click **Change Contribution**
4. Enter new % or $ amount (up to IRS max)
5. Choose Roth vs. Traditional allocation
6. Effective date: Next available pay period
7. Click **Submit**

**Pro Tip:** Increase by 1% annually to painlessly boost retirement savings.

### Change Beneficiaries

1. Navigate to **Benefits**
2. Click **Life Insurance**
3. Click **Update Beneficiaries**
4. Add/remove/edit:
   - Primary beneficiaries (% must total 100%)
   - Contingent beneficiaries (optional)
5. Provide full legal name, DOB, SSN, relationship
6. Click **Submit**

**Important:** Review beneficiaries after major life events (marriage, birth, divorce).

## Performance & Development

### View Performance Goals

1. Navigate to **Performance**
2. Click **My Goals**
3. View active goals set with manager
4. Click goal to update progress or add notes
5. Click **Submit Update**

### Complete Performance Review

1. Check **Inbox** for "Performance Review" notification
2. Click task to open review form
3. Complete self-assessment sections:
   - Goal achievement
   - Core competencies
   - Accomplishments
   - Development areas
4. Click **Submit to Manager**
5. Manager reviews and schedules 1:1 discussion

## Expenses & Reimbursement

### Submit Expense Report

1. Navigate to **Expenses**
2. Click **Create Expense Report**
3. Click **Add Expense**
4. Select expense type (meals, travel, supplies, etc.)
5. Enter:
   - Date of expense
   - Merchant name
   - Amount
   - Business purpose
6. Attach receipt image (required for $25+)
7. Repeat for all expenses
8. Click **Submit** when complete

**Reimbursement:** Direct deposit within 2 weeks of approval.

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Load / Page Error

1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Check workdaystatus.com for outages
5. Still issues? Contact IT: techsupport@company.com or x4357

### Task Stuck "Pending Approval"

- Check with your manager (they may not have seen notification)
- Managers: Check Workday inbox daily
- If 5+ business days, contact HR: hrops@company.com

### Can't Find Something?

Use **Search** bar (top of every Workday page):
- Type task name: "request time off", "view pay", "change address"
- Workday shows matching tasks
- Click to navigate directly
